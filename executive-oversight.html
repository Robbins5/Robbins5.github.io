Executive visibility timeline

Enterprise transition stabilizes in stages. Audit modernization follows enterprise reality—not implementation dates. Here’s what leadership should expect.

Phase A
Operational Stabilization (0–12+ months)
During transitional dual-system states, reporting and controls may span legacy and new environments. Temporary manual bridges increase complexity. Expect controlled volatility while signal integrity and ownership mature before modernization accelerates.
Phase B
Visibility Reinforcement (Year 1–2)
Monitoring routines begin to replace lagging reports. Margin protection, inventory integrity, and pricing discipline gain structured signal coverage.
Phase C
Detection Maturity (Year 2–3)
Exception ownership and escalation become consistent. Leakage surfaces earlier. Audit shifts from periodic validation to signal-driven oversight.

Leadership expectation

Audit modernization is deliberate. Immediate perfection is unrealistic. Earlier detection—not instant redesign—is the measurable sign of progress.
