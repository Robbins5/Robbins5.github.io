The Thesis

Internal audit can’t remain a downstream reviewer while enterprises modernize. Transition creates new failure modes: incomplete stabilization, fractured evidence, new exceptions, and shifting accountability. The thesis lays out why the audit operating model must evolve—toward population signals, monitoring architecture, and real-time assurance.
