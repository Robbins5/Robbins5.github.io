The AI-Ready Audit is a thought leadership platform focused on how internal audit must evolve as AI becomes embedded in enterprise decision-making, automation, and operational workflows. The lens is practical: how governance, controls, evidence, and testing strategy change when systems learn, adapt, and influence outcomes.
