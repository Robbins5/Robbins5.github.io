The platform

The AI-Ready Audit is a thought leadership platform focused on how internal audit evolves as AI becomes embedded in enterprise systems, decision-making processes, and operational workflows.

Governance direction
Accountability

Translating AI capability into roles, responsibilities, oversight, and control expectations.

Control design, evidence requirements, testing strategy, and defensibility in system-driven workflows.

Monitoring-driven oversight
Signals

Moving from periodic sampling to population signals, exception discipline, and continuous routines where feasible.

Why this matters

Traditional audit approaches assume stable systems, predictable logic, and clearly defined workflows. AI-enabled environments challenge those assumptions.

Reality
Systems evolve during operation
Workflows shift, controls lag, and evidence changes shape during transition and adoption.
Risk
Decisions get mediated
AI can recommend, generate, and influence outcomes without explicit programming for every scenario.
Expectation
Standards and stakeholders move
The profession increasingly signals technology-enabled, scalable, risk-based assurance.
