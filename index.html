The backbone

Thesis

As enterprises evolve, internal audit must evolve with them. The constraints that once justified default sampling are changing. Risk increasingly appears between systems, inside temporary workarounds, and through evidence that shifts shape during stabilization. The thesis defines the operating model shift: from periodic validation toward population signals, monitoring architecture, and defensible evidence.
