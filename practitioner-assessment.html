Practitioner Skills Assessment

Where does your skill profile stand today?

Ten questions. A profile across five dimensions. A starting point for an honest development conversation.

This assessment is not a performance review. It is a structured self-reflection on where your skills currently sit across the five dimensions that define the modern audit practitioner. Answer based on where you actually are -- not where you are working toward.

The most useful result is an honest one. Your profile will identify which dimensions are strongest, which represent the biggest opportunity, and which pages in this framework are most relevant to your development right now.
